Abby Ensign, OTR/L

Pediatric Occupational Therapist

About Me

Abby has extensive experience working in both public and private school settings, where she treated students with developmental delays and learning disabilities. Her background in education gives her a deep understanding of the need for collaboration with families and school teams to support children's success.

She particularly enjoys helping children develop handwriting and fine motor skills, and works closely with families to address executive functioning and self-regulation.

Abby holds a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from the University of Michigan and a Master's degree in Occupational Therapy from Touro College, New York. She is trained in various programs, including Learning Without Tears, Size Matters Handwriting Program, and Teacher-Child Interaction Training (TCIT).

Abby lives in Bethesda with her husband, their daughter, and their cavapoo, Archie.

Services

  • Comprehensive Pediatric Occupational Therapy Evaluations and Individualized Treatment Plans
  • Handwriting and Fine Motor Skills Development using Evidence-Based Programs (Learning Without Tears, Size Matters)
  • Executive Functioning and Self-Regulation Strategies for School Success
  • Parent Coaching and Home Program Development for Continued Progress
